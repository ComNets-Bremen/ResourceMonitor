Recent Changes

  • Automatic upload: The data upload is now done automatically. You can disable this behaviour in the settings.
  • Discharge behaviour: The discharge behaviour is now shown more detailed. You can directly see how heavily you used your device recently.
  • Charge / Discharge: The percentage of time you are charging / discharging your device is shown in pie charts.
